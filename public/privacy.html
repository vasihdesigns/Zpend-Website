Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Zpend is built on a simple principle: your financial data is yours. This policy explains exactly what we collect, what we don't, and why.

1. What We Collect

Expense data you enter: Everything you log — amounts, categories, notes — is stored locally on your device using iOS system storage. It does not leave your device.

No personal information: Zpend does not require an account. We never collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers through the App.

Voice input: When you use voice logging, your speech is processed by Apple's on-device Speech framework. Audio is never sent to Zpend or any third-party server.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reporting tools that send data externally
  • No behavioral data or heatmaps

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iPhone using iOS system storage. If you delete Zpend, all data is permanently removed from your device.

If iCloud Backup is enabled on your device, your app data may be included in your personal iCloud backup. This is managed entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Zpend does not integrate analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only system frameworks used are native Apple APIs:

  • Speech framework — for voice expense logging (on-device only)
  • StoreKit — if in-app purchases are offered in the future

5. Children's Privacy

Zpend is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@zpend.app
