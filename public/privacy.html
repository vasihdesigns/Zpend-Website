Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

The short version: Zpend does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers. Your expenses live entirely on your iPhone — or in your own personal iCloud account if you choose to enable backup. We never see your data. No one does, except you.

1. We collect no data

Zpend does not collect any personal information or usage data — at all. This includes:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No financial data or expense amounts
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No crash reports sent to external services
  • No behavioral tracking of any kind

2. Where your data lives

Everything you enter into Zpend — every expense, category, and budget — is stored in one of two places, both fully under your control:

  • On your iPhone — by default, all data is stored locally using iOS system storage on your device
  • Your personal iCloud — if you enable iCloud Backup in the app's settings, your data syncs to your own iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. This is your private iCloud storage, not Zpend's servers

Zpend has no servers. There is no Zpend database, no Zpend cloud, and no Zpend backend that receives or stores your information.

3. No account required

Zpend requires no sign-up, no login, and no account of any kind. You open the app and start tracking immediately. We have no way to identify who you are, and we have no interest in doing so.

4. Voice input

When you use voice logging, your speech is processed entirely by Apple's on-device Speech framework. Audio is processed locally on your iPhone and is never sent to Zpend or any third-party server.

5. Third-party services

Zpend contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising libraries, or tracking tools. The only external framework used is Apple's native Speech framework for on-device voice recognition, which operates entirely on your device.

6. Deleting your data

To delete all your data, simply delete the Zpend app from your iPhone. iOS will permanently remove all locally stored data. If you enabled iCloud Backup, you can remove the Zpend data from your iCloud storage via iPhone Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Storage.

7. Children's privacy

Zpend does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. There is no data collection mechanism in the app at all.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Our commitment to collecting no data will not change.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@zpend.app
