Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Zpend ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Zpend mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Data you enter: Zpend stores your expense entries, categories, budgets, and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use an export or backup feature.

No account required: Zpend does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers through the App itself.

Voice input: When you use the voice logging feature, your spoken input is processed using Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio is not sent to Zpend's servers.

2. How We Use Information

All data you enter into Zpend is used solely to provide the App's functionality — logging expenses, displaying insights, and managing budgets. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored locally on your iPhone using iOS system storage. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

If you use iCloud backup on your device, your App data may be included in your personal iCloud backup. This backup is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Zpend does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. The App may use the following Apple system frameworks:

  • Speech framework — for voice expense logging (on-device processing)
  • StoreKit — if in-app purchases are offered in the future

5. Children's Privacy

Zpend is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
